📋 PROJECT OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

A complete, production-ready billing and invoicing system built with Flask, SQLite,
and ReportLab. The system handles:
- Invoice creation, editing, viewing, and printing
- PDF generation with automatic download to device
- Customer management and sales tracking
- Comprehensive customer ledger reporting
- Payment tracking and reporting
- WhatsApp integration for sharing reports
- Professional invoice printing with multiple copies

🚀 HOW TO USE - STEP BY STEP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. START THE APPLICATION
   $ cd /home/tejas/shop-billing-software
   $ source .venv/bin/activate
   $ python3 start_server.py
   
   Open browser: http://localhost:5000

2. CREATE AN INVOICE
   - Click "Invoices" in navigation
   - Click "Create Invoice" button
   - Select customer
   - Select products and enter quantities
   - System auto-calculates amounts
   - Click "Save Invoice"

3. EDIT AN INVOICE
   - Click "Invoices" in navigation
   - Find the invoice
   - Click "Edit" button (pencil icon)
   - Modify any details:
     * Invoice date
     * PO number
     * Challan number
     * Transport details
     * Products (add/remove/reorder)
     * Quantities and prices
   - See amounts update in real-time
   - Click "Save Changes"

4. DOWNLOAD INVOICE PDF
   - Open invoice list
   - Click "Download PDF" button (green download icon)
   - Browser automatically downloads invoice_{INVOICE_NUMBER}.pdf to your device
   - File saves in Downloads folder

5. PRINT INVOICE
   - Click "Invoices"
   - Click "Print" button (printer icon) OR find invoice and click "View & Print"
   - Select printer or use "Save as PDF"
   - Choose to print 1, 2, or 3 copies

6. VIEW CUSTOMER REPORTS
   - Click "Reports" in navigation
   - Go to "Customer Sales Report" tab
   - See all customers with:
     * Total sales amount
     * Amount paid
     * Amount pending
     * Payment status
   - Click on any customer to see detailed ledger
   - Click "📄 PDF" to download customer report

7. DOWNLOAD CUSTOMER REPORT
   - In "Customer Sales Report" tab
   - Click "📄 Download PDF" button
   - Browser downloads sales_report_{CUSTOMER_NAME}_{DATE}.pdf

8. SHARE CUSTOMER REPORT VIA WHATSAPP
   - In "Customer Sales Report" tab
   - Click on customer to open ledger modal
   - Click "Share on WhatsApp"
   - Opens WhatsApp with pre-filled message including:
     * Customer name
     * Total purchases
     * Total paid
     * Balance due
     * Generation date

🔧 TECHNICAL DETAILS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BACKEND:
- Flask 2.3.0+ (Web framework)
- SQLAlchemy 2.0.0 (ORM)
- SQLite (Database)
- ReportLab (PDF generation)
- Python 3.11

DATABASE:
- Invoice table with fields:
  * invoice_number, invoice_date, customer_id, status
  * po_number, challan_no, transport_mode, vehicle_number
  * contact_person, e_weighment, pay_terms
  * subtotal, total_tax, total_amount, discount
  * created_at, updated_at

- InvoiceItem table with fields:
  * invoice_id, product_id, quantity, unit_price
  * gst_percentage, gst_amount, line_total

- Customer table with complete details
- Product table with 11 products
- Payment table for tracking payments

PDF GENERATION:
- Uses ReportLab library for PDF creation
- Generates PDFs in memory (BytesIO buffer)
- No temporary files needed
- Professional formatting with tables and styling
- Returns with as_attachment=True for automatic download

API ROUTES:
✅ GET  /invoices                    - List all invoices
✅ POST /invoices                    - Create new invoice
✅ GET  /invoices/{id}               - Get invoice details
✅ GET  /edit-invoice/{id}           - Edit invoice page
✅ PUT  /api/invoices/{id}/update    - Update invoice
✅ GET  /api/invoices/{id}/print     - Print invoice
✅ GET  /api/invoices/{id}/download-pdf - Download invoice PDF ✅ FIXED
✅ GET  /api/sales/customers         - List customers
✅ GET  /api/sales/customer/{id}     - Get customer details
✅ GET  /api/sales/customer/{id}/ledger/pdf - Download customer report ✅ FIXED
✅ GET  /reports                     - Reports page
✅ POST /api/sales/customer/{id}/whatsapp-share - WhatsApp share
